Marketing

Digital marketing is the promotion of products or services using online channels and digital technologies, such as the internet, websites, social media, email, and mobile apps. It uses various strategies like search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, and content creation to reach and engage target audiences. Digital marketing provides measurable results and allows for personalized communication, often making it more cost-effective than traditional methods
